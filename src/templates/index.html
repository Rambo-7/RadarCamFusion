Radar Camera Sensor Fusion Demo

Here we use two sensors, a digital camera and a radar system to find and detect people in a scene and provide their range information. The camera uses a pre trained SDD model to find and detect objects. We then use peak detection to identify objects from the radar system and apply a perspective tranformation to the radar data to map the data points onto the camera images.

If the range data point lands in the bounding box of a person, then that point is assigned to that person. Thus we can develop a 3d view of where people are in a given scene through the fusion of these two sensors.
